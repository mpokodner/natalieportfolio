Natalie Marie

About

Natalie Marie is a passionate flutist dedicated to bringing classical music to life through her expressive performances and innovative interpretations.

With over a decade of experience, she has performed with numerous orchestras and chamber ensembles, captivating audiences with her technical precision and emotional depth.

Her repertoire spans from Baroque to contemporary works, showcasing her versatility and commitment to expanding the flute's expressive possibilities.

When not performing, Natalie enjoys teaching and mentoring young musicians, sharing her love for music and helping others discover their musical voice.
